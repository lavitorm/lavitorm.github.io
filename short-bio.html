Short Bio


I’m passionate about problem-solving and exceptional teamwork in environments that are genuinely people-oriented and customer-centric.

Previously graduated with a degree in Communications and MBA with an emphasis in Corporate Finance, I’ve developed a career as an integrative, cross-disciplinary, hands-on multi-project leader, member of Executive Committees, with 10+ yrs in Operations, Strategic Planning, Corporate Development and Finance, in different fields such as Education, Media, Logistics, Internet, E-commerce and Technology.

My passion for teamwork and problem-solving began in projects that I lead with the contribution of razor-sharp multi-disciplinary teams, including world-class consulting firms and investment banks. We drove growth, cut costs, uplifted and ignited results-driven environments fully committed to building sustainable relationships upon values, satisfaction and loyalty.

Today, living in the Bay Area, I’ve shifted my interests to technology and computer science theory related to browser based technologies, databases, deployment and server side development. My education, background in Finance and Project Management have given me a solid base in critical-thinking and problem-solving, upon which I plan to continue to grow my career in hi-tech.

On top of a bias towards curiosity, action, creativity and collaborative work, I’m also an attentive father, world traveler, surfer and a purposeful optimist.
